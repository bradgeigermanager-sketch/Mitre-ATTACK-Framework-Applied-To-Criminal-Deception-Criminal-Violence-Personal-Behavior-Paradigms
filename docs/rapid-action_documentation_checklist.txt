# 📘 **INSTALLMENT 3 — DOCUMENTATION CHECKLIST**  
*A rapid‑action, field‑ready checklist for documenting threats or harm.*

---

# 🟦 **1. Immediate Safety Check**
Before documenting anything, confirm safety.

- **Ensure the scene is safe**  
- **Move victim to a secure location**  
- **Notify security or supervisor if needed**  
- **Do not confront the offender**  

---

# 🟦 **2. Capture Core Incident Details**
Record the essentials **as soon as possible**.

- **Record date and time**  
- **Record exact location**  
- **List all people present**  
- **Note sequence of events**  

---

# 🟦 **3. Document Verbatim Statements**
Write down the offender’s exact words.

- **Capture direct threats word‑for‑word**  
- **Capture implied threats word‑for‑word**  
- **Use quotation marks**  
- **Preserve original spelling/punctuation**  

---

# 🟦 **4. Describe Physical Actions**
Focus on **observable behavior**, not interpretation.

- **Document movements and gestures**  
- **Record proximity and positioning**  
- **Note any physical contact**  
- **Describe object use or manipulation**  

---

# 🟦 **5. Capture Environmental Context**
These details strengthen the credibility of the report.

- **Lighting and visibility**  
- **Noise level or crowding**  
- **Obstacles or blocked exits**  
- **Presence of weapons or tools**  

---

# 🟦 **6. Log Evidence**
Preserve everything **without altering it**.

- **Save messages, screenshots, voicemails**  
- **Photograph damage or injuries**  
- **Collect witness names**  
- **Store originals securely**  

---

# 🟦 **7. Document Victim Impact**
Record **observable** effects only.

- **Note visible emotional reactions**  
- **Record physical symptoms**  
- **Avoid diagnosing or interpreting**  

---

# 🟦 **8. Record Immediate Actions Taken**
This shows responsible response.

- **Who was notified**  
- **Where the victim was moved**  
- **Security or medical involvement**  
- **Any protective measures initiated**  

---

# 🟦 **9. Identify Follow‑Up Needs**
Ensure the incident doesn’t disappear into a void.

- **Security review required**  
- **HR or management follow‑up**  
- **Additional evidence collection**  
- **Victim support or resources**  

---

# 🟦 **10. Final Documentation Review**
Before submitting, confirm:

- **All facts are objective**  
- **All quotes are exact**  
- **All evidence is logged**  
- **No speculation or assumptions included**
