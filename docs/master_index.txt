# 🟦 **1. Reconnaissance**

### Purpose (Behavioral Interpretation)  
The criminal studies the target’s environment, relationships, vulnerabilities, and routines before acting.

---

## **Active Scanning (T1595)**  
**Deceptive Manipulator:** Probes social boundaries with harmless‑seeming questions, small talk, and rapport‑testing.  
**Violent Actor:** Tests physical boundaries — drive‑bys, loitering, probing reactions.

## **Gather Victim Identity Information (T1589)**  
**Manipulator:** Collects personal details to craft tailored manipulation.  
**Violent Actor:** Identifies key individuals to target, intimidate, or neutralize.

## **Gather Victim Network Information (T1590)**  
**Manipulator:** Maps social connections and trust pathways.  
**Violent Actor:** Maps who will intervene, who is vulnerable, and who can be isolated.

## **Gather Victim Org Information (T1591)**  
**Manipulator:** Learns organizational structure to impersonate authority.  
**Violent Actor:** Identifies critical spaces to disrupt or attack.

## **Gather Victim Host Information (T1592)**  
**Manipulator:** Observes routines, workspace habits, and personal environment.  
**Violent Actor:** Identifies ambush points, blind spots, and vulnerable locations.

## **Search Open Websites/Domains (T1593)**  
**Manipulator:** Studies public messaging to mirror language and values.  
**Violent Actor:** Identifies public events or exposed locations.

## **Search Open Technical Databases (T1596)**  
**Manipulator:** Uses public records to uncover leverage.  
**Violent Actor:** Identifies critical infrastructure or weak points.

## **Search Open Repositories (T1597)**  
**Manipulator:** Mines old or obscure information for psychological hooks.  
**Violent Actor:** Finds historical patterns of weak security or response.

## **Search Victim-Owned Websites (T1594)**  
**Manipulator:** Mirrors the victim’s self‑image to gain trust.  
**Violent Actor:** Targets what the victim publicly values most.

## **Phishing for Information (T1598)**  
**Manipulator:** Initiates false‑pretense conversations to extract details.  
**Violent Actor:** Uses coercive questioning to force disclosure.

## **Search Closed Sources (T1599)**  
**Manipulator:** Cultivates insiders to access private information.  
**Violent Actor:** Uses criminal networks to obtain privileged intel.
